Why this blog?

This blog is intended to summarize the information and knowledge I collected around my tech journey on the Internets. It’s an idea around for long time now but I make it real now because I have some free time ahead. I will try hard to keep publish regularly over time. The intent of these posts is to follow the following addage:

  • “learn by doing”
  • “Whatever is well conceived is clearly said, And the words to say it flow with ease."
  • “To teach is to learn twice over."

To summarize, I expect to make knowledge from what I read.
I hope this will also give me a way to see my progress (fingercrossed).
To make it a little harder, I will try to write the post in both French (my native language) and English to keep alive what I’ve learned about this language the last few years.
I suspect these posts won’t be really useful expect to me but who knows?

My interests goes from the embedded dev, the web dev to the infra via the methodology and the architecture.

Good reading!
